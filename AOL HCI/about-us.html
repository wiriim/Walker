    Walker History

    Started as a group of friends that love collecting shoes as hobbies. We realized how hard it is to collect some of the limited edition shoes without buying it for an unreasonable price from resellers. And here we are, still together, loving the same hobbies, and giving other people a chance to love their hobbies at a fair price.

    Walker Vision & Mission

    We strive to give shoe-lovers an easier time finding shoes and buying them for a reasonable price.
